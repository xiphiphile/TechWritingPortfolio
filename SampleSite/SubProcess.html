SubProcess Usage

The subprocess box can be useful for simplifying or decluttering your workflows. This shape stands in for a complex process which can either be called out as a separate flow or in the step-by-step automated process description.

Some good times to use this tool include:

  • You have a complex set of steps that happens repeatedly in your flow.
  • your process strings multiple automations or modules together.
  • Your flow is getting unmanageably large or difficult to follow.
